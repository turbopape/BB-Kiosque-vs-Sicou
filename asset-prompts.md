# Asset Prompts for Nano Banana

All sprites should be PNG with transparent background unless noted.
Keep the child's pencil-drawn style from the base image, but make the pose more aggressive and exaggerated — while staying goofy and funny.

---

## Background (1280x720)

**`assets/background.png`**
> A background in the style of the classic 1987 game "International Karate+" (IK+) by System 3. A beach scene with soft waves, sun reflecting on the sea, a colored sky. The fighters stand on a flat sandy/earthy ground strip in the foreground with the ocean and sky behind them. Keep the retro 8-bit/16-bit aesthetic with clean pixel art and warm nostalgic colors. No characters in the scene — just the backdrop. Size exactly 1280x720 pixels. PNG format, no transparency needed.
